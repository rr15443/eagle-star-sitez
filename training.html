Firearms Training

Required for armed certification

Firearms training is designed for armed security candidates and covers safety, handling, operational use, and practical qualification. Students must demonstrate safe and competent handling and meet qualification requirements to complete the course.

  • Safe handling and firearm fundamentals
  • Range safety and procedures
  • Operational readiness and competency

What to Bring

  • Eye and ear protection

Minimum Requirements (Armed Candidates)

  • Must be at least 21 years old
  • Must be a U.S. citizen or legally eligible to work
  • Must pass required background screening as applicable
